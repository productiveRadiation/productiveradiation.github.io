New Website Design and Game Information

25 July 2022

Hello, netizens! I updated the website with new sections and a blog. I hope this makes it easier to find information about the game.

Also, some people have been wondering what has happened to Cosmo Setter with the abrupt lack of updates. Sorry about that :p

I decided to take the development more private as a lot about the game is constantly changing and evolving. So at this moment, the game is on hold. But I haven't completely stopped work on it. A lot of behind-the-scenes planning and design has been occurring to properly restart the game.

And now, I'll answer some questions in more detail.

Question: What happened to Catco's Galaxy RUST?

I rebranded it as Cosmo Setter, as it makes more sense. And frankly, "Catco's Galaxy RUST" sounds really silly.

Question: Will the game still be released in "chapters", or as a full the game?

Only as a full game. I only chose the "chapters" method to make development of the game faster, but I have realised that going with this style would make the quality between different chapters drastic.

Question: What's the fate of "Fan" characters?

All fan characters submitted via the form in April 2021 scrapped, I simply feel like this would dilute the personal aspect of the game to me. The only characters being used that have been created by another person are ones designed by Caro_.

Question: When do you think the game will be finished?

In 2-4 years. This is a hobby project, so I am taking my time with it.

Thank you for reading article.
