Cosmo Setter is a side-scrolling action platforming video game. Explore the universe and help a cat child save it from an evil calamity. You'll explore different environments and characters that will help you on your journey.


FEATURES

  • Platforming gameplay based around exploration and environments.
  • An interesting story that goes beyond just saving the world.
  • Multiple friends and foes to meet.
